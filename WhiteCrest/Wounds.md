Wounds occur in degrees, eventually culminating in death
## Variation 1
- 1° - -1 to all rolls
- 2° - Roll minor wound
- 3° - Roll major wound
- 4° - Dying
- 5° - Deaths door
- 6° - Dead
## Variation 2
- 1° - -1 to all rolls
- 2° - Roll minor wound
- 3° - Roll major wound
- 4° - Roll major wound x 2
- 5° - Roll major wound x (X°-2)
## Variation 3
Roll on wound table d20 + (X°-1)
